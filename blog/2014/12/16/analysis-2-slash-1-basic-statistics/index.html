Discussion

The graph with average benefit change does not seem very interesting - the benefit just increases and looks quite different from the one produced by Evo in chalprop-matlab simulation with the same parameters (see presentation, page 9). Also, the average degree and number of knowsLinks in the graph is somewhat suspicious: 2.5 is way too little for what you would expect from 200,000 situations and 100 iterations. There are two possibilities as always - some sort of bug preventing the simulation run correctly or… feature of the algorithm.

Anyway, more checking needed. I’d say:

  • first of all check the distribution of situation to the agents (is it random);
  • degree distributions and weight distributions…
