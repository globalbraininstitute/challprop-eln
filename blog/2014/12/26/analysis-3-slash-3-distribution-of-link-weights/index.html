Analysis 3/3: Distribution of Link Weights

Date: Sat Dec 27 13:04:50 2014
Analysis version (challprop-analytics): bf1470b
(use git checkout $commit$ if needed)

Back to the experiment page

First, the number of links between agents in the graph: 19877. The graph is sparse (average degree 1.99) given that we have 10k of agents. Further, 98% of all link weights are between -5 and 55:

##         1%        10%        20%        30%        50%        70% 
## -5.1121464 -0.2052084  0.4435458  1.0102844  2.1798460  3.9991478 
##        90%        99% 
##  9.7597988 54.7315925

Link weights are clearly biased towards positive: only 0.1263772 of all links have negative or zero weight. A density plot of 98% of link weights (i.e. between -5 and 55) shows that the distribution probably can be called a “power-law with a bias towards positive”:

This shows that most of the links are positive but very small.
